I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering and the Department of Computer Science at Louisiana State University. I obtained Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Virginia, where I worked with Prof. Homa Alemzadeh. I received my M.Eng in Control Science and Engineering and my B.Eng in Automation from Shandong University. Before joining UVA in 2019, I was a Senior Engineer at NR Research Institute, State Grid of China.

My research interests are at the intersection of computer systems dependability and control system theory& engineering. I am particularly interested in engineering safer autonomous systems through resilience assessment, security validation, and artificial intelligence techniques.

My lab has openings for highly motivated Ph.D. students and Graduate/Undergraduate research interns. If you are interested in working with me, please send me your CV, transcript, and research vision.
